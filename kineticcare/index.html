Questions

Straight answers.

The things patients actually ask before booking their first assessment.

How long does physiotherapy take to show results at KineticCare?+

Most KineticCare patients see measurable improvement within 4 weeks of starting their evidence-based protocol. Chronic conditions like lumbar disc herniation typically take 8–12 weeks; post-surgical rehab such as ACL reconstruction averages 16–20 weeks to return to full activity. Median recovery at KineticCare is 3.2× faster than conventional physiotherapy, based on 12,427 completed programs.

What does biomechanics AI motion analysis involve?+

AI motion capture records your gait, posture, and joint kinematics during your first consultation. The system flags asymmetries, movement compensations, and force-plate readings that your physiotherapist then uses to design a precision loading program — sets, reps, and progressions calibrated specifically to your biomechanics.

Is the first assessment really free?+

Yes. KineticCare offers a free 20-minute assessment at our Indiranagar clinic with no credit card required and no commitment to continue. You will receive a clear diagnosis and a recommended recovery path.

Do you treat patients outside Bengaluru?+

Our Pro Recovery plan includes live video consultations with real-time form correction for patients anywhere in India. On-site sessions are currently available at our Indiranagar clinic in Bengaluru.

How much does physiotherapy at KineticCare cost?+

Plans start at ₹2,499 per month for Essential (4 video consultations, exercise library access). Pro Recovery is ₹4,999 per month and includes 8 sessions with AI motion analysis. Corporate plans for teams of 20 or more are custom-quoted. Every plan begins with a free 20-minute assessment and has no lock-in.

What conditions can KineticCare physiotherapists treat?+

KineticCare treats spine and posture conditions (disc herniation, scoliosis, chronic neck and back pain), sports injuries (ACL, rotator cuff, ligament tears), chronic pain through pain neuroscience, neurological conditions (stroke recovery, Parkinson's), women's health (pelvic floor, prenatal, diastasis recti), and delivers corporate ergonomics programs for Bengaluru tech companies.
